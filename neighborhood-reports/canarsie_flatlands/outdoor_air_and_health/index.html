Outdoor Air and Health in
Canarsie - Flatlands

Air pollution is one of the most important environmental threats to urban populations and while all people are exposed, pollutant emissions, levels of exposure, and population vulnerability vary across neighborhoods. Exposures to common air pollutants have been linked to respiratory and cardiovascular diseases, cancers, and premature deaths.

Canarsie - Flatlands includes ZIP Codes: 11234,11236,11239.

What the city is doing?
  • Enforces tenant and housing laws: landlords must keep homes free of pests and mold
  • Offers free Healthy Homes Community Workshops
  • Monitors air quality across neighborhoods through the NYC Community Air Survey
  • Follows a plan called OneNYC to create a more sustainable and equitable City
What can you do?
  • Work with your healthcare provider to create an asthma action plan
  • Check the weather report for the air quality index and pollen counts
  • If you smoke, quit. Don’t allow smoking in your home or car
  • Reduce dust levels in your home. Removing clutter and cleaning regularly can help.
  • Fix leaks in your home to prevent mold. Notify your landlord. If they do not fix leaks promptly, call 311.

Indicator ranking

Each indicator is placed in one of three categories:

  • Better
  • Middle
  • Worse

This shows us how the neighborhood compares to other NYC neighborhoods, if you ranked all of the NYC neighborhoods based on this indicator.

  • Better: in the top 1/3rd of all NYC neighorhoods.
  • Middle: it's close to the NYC median - ranking somewhere in the middle third of all neighborhoods.
  • Worse: in the bottom 1/3rd of NYC neighborhoods.

These are only based on the distribution of data values, not the range. The absolute values of indicators in different categoeis may not be all that different.
